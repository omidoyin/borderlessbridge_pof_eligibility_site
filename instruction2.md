I personally recommend having the name of the event in the email be:

{Scheduler's_First_Name} & BorderlessBridge: Proof of Funds (POF) Strategy Call

This form, just before they confirm their booking is meant to qualify for:

- Budget (do they have money for this service?), 
- Authority (are they ones controling the final decision and the money?), 
- Need (is it something that's high priority?), and 
- Time (is now the best time to get started?)


The form on the main page delved deep into the Need and Time aspects. 
That leaves Budget and Authority for the call booking form.


In addition to those two questions, you should that that question that's like: 

- We have packages ranging from ₦1.5M as high as ₦5.5M. Which of these describes you best? (And you give those options) This single handedly qualifies for budget. It should be the second question, with the current first question being the third question (as it builds up on this very one)

- The first question should be the Authority Check, so something like: Who are the primary people who need this POF? (Add options like Just me, Me and my spouse, and other possible answers) Then put it in brackets that everyone who can influence the final decision make themselves available on the call, so we can move fast ahead if the service is the right fit.


Instead of putting the budget question on the booking form, put it on the MAIN form on the page.
So that those who can't pay don't even get the opportunity to book.
