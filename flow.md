This is actually a very smart, battle-tested tactic called a Chat-to-Page Loop. It completely solves your budget problem because it lets you buy the cheap traffic via WhatsApp ads, but uses the landing page as an automated "bouncer" to kick out the time-wasters.

If you drop a landing page link immediately as an automated reply, it changes the dynamic completely. Here is why it works, the risks you need to manage, and exactly how to execute it so you don't lose your best leads.

Why This Strategy Works (The Pros)
You Get Cheap Meta Traffic: You are still running a "Click-to-WhatsApp" campaign, meaning your ad spend stays low, and Meta rewards you with high volume.

Automation Does the Heavy Lifting: The moment they click your ad, your WhatsApp automated greeting instantly fires back with the link. Your team doesn’t lift a finger.

The Ultimate Filter: Unserious people are incredibly lazy. If they aren't willing to click a link and read your terms or fill out a quick form, they were never going to transfer you money for a POF service anyway. They will ghost the chat, leaving your inbox clean.

The Danger: The "Ghosting" Effect (The Cons)
You must expect a massive drop-off. If 100 people message you on WhatsApp and your bot immediately replies with a link, only about 30 to 40 people will actually click it. People open WhatsApp because they want to talk to a human. If they wanted to browse a website, they would have used Google. Sending a link right away can feel cold, robotic, or like a bait-and-switch.

How to Set It Up So It Actually Converts
To stop people from running away when they see the link, you cannot just drop a raw URL. You have to frame it so they see it as a benefit, not homework.

❌ The Wrong Way (High Ghost Rate):
"Hello. Please click this link to see our prices and terms before talking to us: www.yourlandingpage.com"
(Feels like a robotic rejection; users will close the app).

The Right Way (High Conversion Rate):
Set up your WhatsApp automated away/greeting message to frame the link as an "Instant Quote Calculator" or a "Fast-Track" tool.

"Hi there! 👋 Thanks for reaching out to us. >
To give you your 2.5% POF quotation instantly without making you wait for an agent to type, tap our secure fast-track link below:

🔗 [www.yourlandingpage.com/quote]

It takes 30 seconds to select your target country and see your processing fee. Once submitted, your dedicated account manager will instantly activate your file here on WhatsApp!"

What the Landing Page Needs to Do
Since they are leaving WhatsApp to go to this page, the page needs to do its filtering job quickly. It should feature:

The Core Terms: Explicitly state your 2.5% rate (or whatever your structure is) and processing timelines so there are no surprises.

Social Proof: Show those redacted visa approval letters (like the Astral Trail ad) to prove you are legitimate.

The Trap Door (The Form): Have a short form asking for their Name, Target Country, and Needed Amount.

The Loop Back: The "Submit" button on that form should redirect them back to WhatsApp with a pre-filled text saying: "Hi, I just submitted my POF request on your site for [Amount] to [Country]. Please review my file."

The Verdict
Do it. It is the absolute best way to protect a small ad budget while protecting your team's sanity. Just make sure the automated message dressing up the link sounds friendly, professional, and helpful.
